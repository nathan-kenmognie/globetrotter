How To Get Around

Cameroon offers various transportation options for getting around the country, including:

  • Public Buses: Affordable and widely used for intercity travel.
  • Taxis: Available in cities, with both shared and private options.
  • Bikes and Motorbikes: Common in urban areas for short distances.
  • Car Rentals: Available in major cities for those who prefer driving.
  • Domestic Flights: Convenient for long distances, connecting major cities.
